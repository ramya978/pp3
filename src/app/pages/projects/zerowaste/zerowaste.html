ZWIC, Zero Waste India Collective

ZWIC is a volunteer driven organization by a group of women formed to facilitate recycling of all kinds of dry and recyclable waste and thus contribute towards cleaner and greener environment. Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)

Extended Producer Responsibility is a concept where a producer's responsibility for a product is extended to the post-consumer stage at a product's life ends. It requires producers to take responsibility for the environmental impacts of their products throughout the product life-cycle, including at end-of-life.

EPR is an environmental policy approach in which a producer's responsibility for a product is extended to the post-consumer stage at a product's life end. It places greater responsibility for the environmental impacts of their products throughout the product's life, including at end-of-life, focusing more on environmental impacts that must be considered as environmental accountability for environment impacts that need to be addressed by design.
